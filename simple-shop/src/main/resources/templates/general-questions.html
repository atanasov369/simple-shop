1) I would like to return a gift that was sent to me. How do I do that?

Eshop is very sorry that your gift did not work for you. Please call the Eshop Customer Loyalty Team for assistance returning the item.

2) How long does it take for me to get a refund?

For the vast majority of returns, it takes about 5-10 Business Days for us to get your package at the fulfillment center, inspect your return, process it into inventory, and complete your refund. If the refund is being issued to a credit card, depending on your credit card company, it may take an additional 2-10 Business Days after your credit is applied for it to post to your account.

3) Do you do back orders?

No. We do not do back orders. If an item is out of stock the Eshop Customer Loyalty Team will notify you as soon as possible and ask if you would like a replacement item. Out of stock orders will be cancelled unless a replacement item is requested.

4) Is the item I want going to go on sale soon?

Unfortunately, Eshop does not know if an item will go on sale or be marked down until it actually happens.
